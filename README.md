
# #Online Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

Tools: Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Excel
Skills: Data cleaning, star-schema data modeling, KPI creation, customer segmentation, geographic analysis

# Key Achievements:

ー　Designed and deployed a full interactive Online Retail Analytics Dashboard in Power BI to analyze revenue, profit, customer loyalty, and product demand across 30+ countries.

ー　Built a star-schema data model with fact and dimension tables, improving report performance, relationship clarity, and DAX calculation accuracy.

ー　Cleaned and transformed 500K+ rows of raw sales data using Power Query by correcting negative quantities, invalid unit prices, and duplicate invoices.

ー　Created key KPIs including Total Revenue ($9.83M), Total Profit ($3.93M), Profit Margin (40%), and Quantity Sold (0.49M).

ー　Developed time-series visuals revealing strong seasonal revenue peaks, with November showing the highest demand due to holiday shopping.

ー　Conducted customer segmentation to identify high-value and high-frequency customers, enabling targeted retention strategy recommendations.

ー　Analyzed international markets and highlighted high-opportunity regions, particularly the Netherlands and EIRE, to support expansion decisions.

ー　Delivered product-level insights by ranking high-revenue SKUs (e.g., DOTCOM POSTAGE, PAPER CRAFT), enhancing inventory and marketing focus.
